Spreadsheet Wars

Premiere Pro

This assignment was to create a video collage in Premiere Pro, using footage from Archive.org I found some great footage of the game Team Fortress Two, and an old show about computers called The Computer Chronicles.

One of the episodes of The Computer Chronicles was about the recent competition in the spreadsheet market, and it took the rather intimidating title of "The spreadsheet wars". I thought this over exagerated name would pair greatly with the footage of soldiers fighting in Team Fortress Two, so I combined them into the collage you see above.
